Why We Do This

In Nigeria, Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) make up approximately 15% of the population, facing significant barriers to inclusion in societal structures due to stigma, limited accessibility, and inadequate resources. Creating an inclusive world demands recognizing the inherent value and potential of PWDs, prioritizing their needs, and advocating for their rights.

By fostering awareness and understanding, we can break down barriers, promote equal opportunities, and celebrate the unique perspectives and talents of PWDs. Embracing inclusion not only benefits PWDs but enriches society as a whole, fostering innovation, diversity, and empathy.

Together, let's champion inclusivity and ensure that everyone, regardless of ability, has the opportunity to thrive, building a more equitable and compassionate society for generations to come.
